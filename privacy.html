We ask that you read this privacy notice carefully, as it contains important information on who we are, how and why we collect, store, use, and share personal information, your rights in relation to your personal information and how to contact us and supervisory authorities in the event you have a complaint.

1. Who we are

Lazy Tiger Labs, LLC (“Lazy Tiger Labs,” “We,” or “Us”) collects, uses and is responsible for certain personal information about you as part of providing the Lazy Tiger Labs website and mobile applications (collectively, the “Service”).

For users located in the European Union, we are regulated under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and act as the data controller for personal information processed under those laws.

2. The Personal Information we collect and use

Information collected by us

  • IP address, browser type, gameplay data, usage activity, and other technical information collected automatically while you use the Service ("Analytic Information"). Legal basis: GDPR Art. 6(1)(b).
  • Your device’s Advertising ID (such as Google Advertising ID or Apple IDFA). Legal basis: GDPR Art. 6(1)(f).
  • Information related to in-app purchases, such as purchase history, to provide purchased content. We do not collect or store payment card details. All transactions are processed by the applicable app store. Legal basis: GDPR Art. 6(1)(b).

How we use your Personal Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • To analyze aggregate usage and gameplay behavior.
  • To display advertisements, including personalized advertisements.

Who we share your Personal Information with

We share personal information with trusted third parties that help us operate and improve the Service. Some data may be collected directly by third parties through SDKs integrated into the Service.

Other disclosures

We may disclose personal information if we believe in good faith that such action is necessary to comply with applicable law, legal process, or governmental requests, to enforce our policies, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Lazy Tiger Labs or others.

Data retention

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide the Service and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

Children’s privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete such information.

3. Transfer of your Information out of the EEA

Lazy Tiger Labs is based in the United States. Your information may be transferred to, processed, and stored in the United States or other countries where data protection laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

4. EU Residents – Your Rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to the processing of your personal data, and to request data portability.

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@lazytigerlabs.com.

5. California Residents – Your Rights

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have the right to know what personal information is collected, request deletion of personal information, and opt out of the sale of personal information.

Do Not Sell My Personal Information: Lazy Tiger Labs does not sell personal information.

California residents may submit a request by visiting our California Privacy Rights page or by contacting us at support@lazytigerlabs.com.

6. Data request verification

To protect your privacy, we may require information to verify your identity when processing data access or deletion requests. This may include your Advertising ID and device type.

7. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect personal information, including encryption and access controls.

8. How to complain

If you have concerns about our use of your personal information, please contact us first so we can address your concerns. EU residents also have the right to lodge a complaint with their local supervisory authority.
